Keybinds/Macros as of v0.1.2:

Shift+V - Repair Vehicle your mouse is over (taps e then clicks)
Shift+R - Spam R
Shift+ScrollDown - Select all idle units
Shift+G - Quickly clicks camera around minimap for some map knowledge
Shift+H - Slowly clicks camera around minimap to allow for quick changes
Shift+F - My standard Soviet Opening (on high resources, Conscript, Molotovs, Conscript. Might add in the Engineer select and move too)
Shift+Z - Opens tactical map because I dont have a numpad
Shift+C - Conscript Spam
